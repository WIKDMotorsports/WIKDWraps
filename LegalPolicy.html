3. General Service Refund Policy

Because WIKD Wraps provides custom vehicle services — including paint protection film, window tint, vinyl wrap, ceramic coating, paint correction, and detailing — refunds are limited.

WIKD Wraps stands behind the quality of its workmanship. If installation quality is determined by WIKD Wraps to fall outside its internal quality control metrics or reasonable industry standards, WIKD Wraps may offer complimentary repairs, corrections, adjustments, or replacements at its discretion.

WIKD Wraps does not offer a separate or formal Company warranty unless expressly provided in a written agreement signed by an authorized representative of WIKD Wraps. Product warranties, if available, are provided by the product manufacturer and are subject to the manufacturer's own terms, conditions, compliance rules, registration requirements, maintenance intervals, exclusions, and claim approval process.

Refunds are only considered after all of the following conditions are met:

  • The client timely reports a valid service concern within the inspection and rework request period
  • The client attends the complimentary two-week follow-up or submits a written rework request within 14 calendar days after the scheduled follow-up appointment
  • The client allows WIKD Wraps a reasonable opportunity to inspect the vehicle
  • WIKD Wraps is given a reasonable opportunity to correct, repair, adjust, replace, or otherwise address the concern
  • The client remains dissatisfied with the outcome after WIKD Wraps has attempted the available remedy
Even after these steps, refunds are not automatic. WIKD Wraps reserves the right to determine, in its discretion, whether a refund, partial refund, material-only refund, store credit, additional correction, paid repair, no-cost repair, replacement, or denial is appropriate based on the circumstances. If a refund is approved, the refund may be limited to material costs only. Labor costs are non-refundable if the installation or service meets WIKD Wraps' quality control metrics or reasonable industry standards.

4. No Refunds for Subjective Dissatisfaction

Refunds will not be granted for:

  • Normal wear, aging, or degradation over time
  • Subjective aesthetic concerns unrelated to defects
  • Preference changes after approval of the work
  • Buyer's remorse
  • Failure to follow aftercare or maintenance instructions
  • Damage from improper washing, harsh chemicals, road debris, accidents, or third-party work
  • Issues from pre-existing vehicle defects, prior repairs, or hidden vehicle conditions

5. Inspection & Two-Week Follow-Up

A complimentary two-week follow-up appointment will be offered at vehicle pick-up. This allows WIKD Wraps to inspect the completed work, review curing concerns, and address any installation-quality issues.

If the client fails to attend the follow-up or submit a written rework request within 14 calendar days after the scheduled follow-up, WIKD Wraps will not consider a refund request related to installation quality or service outcome.

6. PPF, Vinyl Wrap & Window Tint

These are custom services performed for your specific vehicle. Once installed, materials generally cannot be reused or returned. The following are not automatic grounds for refund:

  • Minor dust, debris, or bubbles during normal curing
  • Seams, relief cuts, or multi-piece installations required by panel design
  • Material shrinkage or tension related to panel complexity
  • Damage from road debris, accidents, improper washing, or general wear
  • Subjective dissatisfaction unrelated to defects

7. Ceramic Coating Refunds

Ceramic coatings are not permanent, scratch-proof, or maintenance-free. Refunds will not be granted for coating failure caused by improper care, unauthorized chemicals, or failure to follow manufacturer maintenance protocols.

8. Detailing & Paint Correction

Results vary based on vehicle condition, paint type, defect depth, and selected service level. WIKD Wraps does not guarantee full removal of every scratch, stain, or imperfection. Refunds will not be granted because a defect could not be fully removed when full removal was not safely achievable.

9. Repairs, Removals, Adhesive Removal & Reinstallation

Film, tint, vinyl, adhesive, badge, decal, or coating removal may reveal or cause damage, especially on vehicles with prior repaint, poor bodywork, aged paint, clear coat failure, adhesive promoter use, prior installations by other shops, long-term sun exposure, or unknown installation methods.

Adhesive residue removal may be billed separately to the client.

WIKD Wraps reserves the right to refuse refunds, free replacements, or installation-quality warranty claims related to adhesive residue, removal complications, or damage caused by pre-existing vehicle conditions, prior installations, non-factory paint, or work performed by another shop.

Vehicles with films, wraps, tint, coatings, badges, or adhesive installed by other shops may carry additional removal and repair risk. Additional charges may apply.

10. Disassembly Labor

Unless expressly included in writing, disassembly and reassembly labor is not included in standard pricing. Refunds will not be granted for clip failure, brittle component failure, or issues from aged or aftermarket parts unless caused by proven negligence by WIKD Wraps.

11. Deposits, Cancellations & Rescheduling

A deposit may be required for appointments exceeding $1,000, custom work, or special-order materials. Unless otherwise agreed in writing:

  • Deposits are applied toward the final invoice
  • Cancellations within 48 hours of the appointment may forfeit the deposit
  • Rescheduling must be requested at least 48 hours in advance
  • No-shows may result in forfeiture of the deposit
  • Special-order or vehicle-specific materials may be non-refundable once ordered
