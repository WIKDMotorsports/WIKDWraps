How To Use
1. Paste Shopify CDN URL for the image.
2. Click a tag type (e.g. PPF, Stage 1).
3. Click the image to place the tag.
4. Drag dots to adjust position.
5. Click BAKE to save and clear.
6. Repeat for next image.
7. Copy final JSON list at the bottom.
