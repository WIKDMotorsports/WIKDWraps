What is PPF?

Paint Protection Film (PPF), also known as a clear bra or protective film, is a thin, self-healing thermoplastic polyurethane layer applied to your vehicle’s painted surfaces. With a thickness of 7.5-12 mils, it provides advanced protection against rock chips, scratches, and chemical contaminants. The UV-resistant adhesive ensures a secure bond to your paint, helping preserve your vehicle’s finish for years to come.
